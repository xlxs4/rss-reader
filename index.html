    Fractional Collocation Method for Third-Kind Volterra Integral Equations with Nonsmooth Solutions
    Abstract In this paper, we develop a collocation method for solving third-kind Volterra integral equations. For the proposed method, we derive an error estimate in the \(L^\infty \) -norm, which shows that the optimal order of global convergence can be obtained by choosing the appropriate parameter \(\lambda \) and modified mesh, even when the exact solution has low regularity.
