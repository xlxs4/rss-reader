    SIAM Journal on Applied Dynamical Systems, Volume 22, Issue 4, Page 3059-3092, December 2023. Abstract.     SIAM Journal on Applied Dynamical Systems, Volume 22, Issue 4, Page 2991-3024, December 2023. Abstract.
