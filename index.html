    SIAM Journal on Applied Mathematics, Volume 83, Issue 1, Page 194-224, February 2023. Abstract. Many physical, epidemiological, or physiological dynamical processes on networks support front-like propagation, where an initial localized perturbation grows and systematically invades all nodes in the network.
